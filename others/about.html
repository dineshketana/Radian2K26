ABOUT RADIAN 2K26

Igniting Innovation, Empowering Technology

RADIAN 2K26 is the flagship National Level Technical & Cultural Fest organized by the Department of Information Technology, ANITS, Visakhapatnam.

The fest brings together innovators, developers, designers, and creative minds from across the nation through hackathons, coding contests, workshops, poster presentations, and cultural events.

ABOUT IT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Information Technology at ANITS emphasizes strong foundations in computing along with exposure to AI, data science, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and modern web technologies.

By integrating academics with hands-on learning, the department prepares students with industry-ready skills and a mindset of continuous learning.

ABOUT ANITS

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technologies and Sciences (ANITS) has upheld excellence in technical education for over two decades. The institution is known for academic discipline, innovation, and holistic student development.

ANITS fosters entrepreneurship, research, and social responsibility, empowering students to contribute meaningfully to society.
