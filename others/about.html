ABOUT RADIAN 2K26

Where Technology Meets Creativity

RADIAN 2K26 is the flagship National Level Technical & Cultural Fest presented by the Department of Information Technology at ANITS, Visakhapatnam — an electrifying platform where innovation, imagination, and competition converge. Through engaging events like hackathons, coding battles, poster exhibits, design challenges, and cultural showcases, we empower participants to unleash their creative potential and technical prowess.

The festival celebrates not only technological brilliance but also artistic expression, fostering a holistic environment for future innovators.

ABOUT IT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Information Technology at ANITS fosters intellectual growth, technical insight, and research exploration. Emphasizing hands-on learning, students are equipped to tackle real challenges in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Cloud Technology, and Software Development. RADIAN gives them a stage to showcase those capabilities.

ABOUT ANITS

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technologies and Sciences (ANITS) stands as a beacon of quality technical education in South India. For over 25 years, ANITS has consistently produced industry-ready engineers, innovators, and leaders known for excellence in academics, research, and social impact.

With a supportive ecosystem for startups, laboratory research, and professional partnerships, ANITS continues to shape the future of technology and entrepreneurship.
