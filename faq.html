FAQ's

Everything you need to know about RADIAN 2K26

What is RADIAN 2K26? General

+
RADIAN 2K26 is a national-level technical and cultural fest featuring tech events, workshops, competitions, and non-tech activities.

Who can participate? Eligibility

+
Students from any college and any branch are eligible to participate unless specified otherwise for a particular event.

Is registration free? Registration

+
Some events are free while others have minimal registration fees. Details are available on the respective event pages.

Will certificates be provided? Certificates

+
Yes, all participants will receive participation certificates and winners will receive merit certificates.

Whom should I contact for queries? Support

+
You can reach out to the event coordinators listed on the Coordinators page or use the Contact section for official queries.
